The Post-graduate fee hike

"On 19th July 2022, about 100 students from the institute gathered in the main building to protest against the recent massive fee hike. The gathered students aimed to submit an open letter signed by 1000 students to the director stating some student demands concerning the fees hike. However, security personnel stopped the group on the ground floor. The group continued their protest on the ground floor and later handed over the open letter to the two Deputy Directors and the Registrar of the institute, who came to address the protesting students."
